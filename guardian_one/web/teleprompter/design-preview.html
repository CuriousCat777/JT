Empathy & Autonomy
Use the NURSE framework: Name the emotion ("I can see this is overwhelming"), Understand their perspective, Respect their values, Support them, and Explore what matters most. When family and patient disagree on goals, facilitate a family meeting using the "I wish...I worry...I wonder" framework.
Telehealth Presence
Position your camera at eye level. Lean slightly forward. Use the patient's name at least 3 times. Pause after delivering information — silence is powerful on video. Avoid looking at notes; use the teleprompter for key talking points.
Cultural Sensitivity
Ask: "Is there anything about your background, culture, or beliefs that would help me take better care of you?" Avoid assumptions. Use professional interpreters for language barriers — never family members for medical translation.
