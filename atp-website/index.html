About

Bridging Clinical Practice & Academic Excellence

Dr. Jeremy Tabernero founded Academic Thought Projects to bridge the gap between clinical expertise and academic productivity. With a background in medicine and a passion for scholarly inquiry, he helps professionals turn their knowledge and experience into meaningful academic contributions.

Whether you're a seasoned researcher seeking strategic direction or a clinician exploring your first publication, ATPs provides the structure, mentorship, and expertise to bring your academic vision to life.

  • 📚
    Research Strategy End-to-end project design and manuscript development
  • 🌱
    Mentorship-First Approach Empowering you to grow as an independent scholar
  • 🧬
    Clinical-Academic Integration Making academic work practical and patient-centered
